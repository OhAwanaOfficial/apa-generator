How to Cite a Website in APA Format

To cite a website in APA format, include the author, year, title of the page, and URL. This method works for online articles, blogs, and web pages used in academic writing.

APA Website Citation Format

Author Last Name, First Initial. (Year). Title. Retrieved from URL

Example of APA Website Citation

Smith, J. (2023). Understanding AI. Retrieved from https://example.com
