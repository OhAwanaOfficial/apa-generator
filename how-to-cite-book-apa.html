How to Cite a Book in APA Format

To cite a book in APA format, include the author name, publication year, book title, and publisher. This applies to textbooks and academic books used by students and others.

APA Book Citation Format

Author Last Name, First Initial. (Year). Book Title. Publisher.

Example of APA Book Citation

Smith, J. (2020). Introduction to Psychology. Oxford University Press.
