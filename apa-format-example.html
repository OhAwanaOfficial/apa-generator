APA Format Example for Students

APA format is used in academic writing to cite sources like books, websites, and journals. Below are simple APA format examples for students and others.

APA Book Example

Smith, J. (2020). Introduction to Psychology. Oxford University Press.

APA Website Example

Smith, J. (2023). Understanding AI. Retrieved from https://example.com

APA Journal Example

Smith, J. (2021). AI in education. Journal of Modern Learning.
