How to Cite a Journal in APA Format

To cite a journal in APA format, include the author name, publication year, article title, and journal name. This format is used for academic research papers and scholarly articles.

APA Journal Citation Format

Author Last Name, First Initial. (Year). Article title. Journal Name.

Example of APA Journal Citation

Smith, J. (2021). The impact of artificial intelligence on education. Journal of Modern Learning.
